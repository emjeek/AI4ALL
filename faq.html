Common questions

You do not need to know where to start.

Most people do not need more AI news or another overwhelming list of apps. They need a patient, practical conversation about what would actually make life or work easier.

Do I need to understand AI before booking?

No. Starting from scratch is completely fine. My job is to make the confusing parts clear and help you focus on what is useful.

Is this only for businesses?

No. AI4ALL is for everyday people, small businesses, teams, schools, churches, and community organisations.

Will AI replace the way we already work?

The goal is not to throw everything out. We build simple improvements around the work you already do, especially the repetitive parts.

Can you train a whole team?

Yes. Team sessions are beginner-friendly and focused on practical examples people can use immediately.

What happens in a clarity call?

We talk through your situation, find the most useful opportunities, and work out the simplest next step. No jargon and no pressure.
